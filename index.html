TreeLogger
TreeLogger is an application used for monitoring surveys of trees growing on a user's properties. With TreeLogger, users can create and manage multiple properties within their account, add and manage trees on those properties, and conduct surveys of their trees. By reviewing past surveys, users can obtain valuable insights into tree growth rates, health patterns, and various other qualities.
